Gobbinsite is a rare zeolite belonging to the gismondine group with ideal chemical formula between Na5(Al5Si11O32)ú11H2O and Na4Ca(Al6Si10O32)ú11H2O, Z =1 [1,2] and references therein, which can be found as late hydrothermal product in basalt cavities often in close association with phillippsite [3?7].
