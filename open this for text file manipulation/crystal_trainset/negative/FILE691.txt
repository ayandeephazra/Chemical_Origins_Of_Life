CEL has four known polymorphs (Forms I to IV) (Ferro and Miyake, 2009; Lu et al., 2006), an amorphous form (Wang and Sun, 2019), sodium salt hydrates (Guzman et al., 2007; Remenar et al., 2011), several solvates (Bond and Sun, 2020; Ferro and Miyake, 2009), and cocrystals with both common cocrystal formers (Almarsson et al., 2007; Bolla et al., 2014; Remenar et al., 2007; Salaman et al., 2011b; Wang et al., 2014; Zhang et al., 2017) and drugs (Almansa et al., 2017; Salaman et al., 2011a).
