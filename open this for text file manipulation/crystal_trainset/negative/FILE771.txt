Formulation A also has 23% MCC (as the plastic binder to enhance tablet strength), mannitol Pearlitol 100SD (to enhance the flow of the formulation) (Paul et al., 2018), crospovidone (as a disintegrant) (Gordon et al., 1993), SLS (as a dual functionality wetting agent and lubricant) (Dun et al., 2018), and MgSt (as a lubricant) (Ertel and Carstensen, 1988).
