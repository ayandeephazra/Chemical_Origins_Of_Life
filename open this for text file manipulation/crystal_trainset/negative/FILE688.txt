Common crystal engineering approaches include cocrystallization (Almarsson and Zaworotko, 2004; Chattoraj et al., 2010; Cherukuvada et al., 2016; Dai et al., 2018; Duggirala et al., 2016; Paul et al., 2019; Sun, 2013), salt formation (Serajuddin, 2007) and hydrate formation (Healy et al., 2017; Hilfiker and Raumer, 2018; Sun, 2009; Sun and Grant, 2004).
