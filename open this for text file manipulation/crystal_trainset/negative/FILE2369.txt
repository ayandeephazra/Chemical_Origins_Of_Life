Garnets are widespread minerals in the upper mantle and transition zone of Earth, comprising of up to 40% of the pyrolite composition volume and up to 70% of the eclogitic composition (Akaogi and Akimoto, 1977; Anderson and Bass, 1984; Irifune and Ringwood, 1987; Ita and Stixrude, 1992; Dymshits et al., 2014).
