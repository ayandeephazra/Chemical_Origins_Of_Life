In general, the steps involved can be categorized into the following steps: (a) evaporation: that includes (i) thermal evaporation (ii) electron beam evaporation and (iii) molecular beam epitaxy, (b) sputtering: can be categorized by (i) DC sputter (ii) radio frequency sputter (iii) magnetron sputter, (c) ion Plating and (d) pulsed laser deposition.
